      PRIVACY POLICY
    

    

  


  

    

      

        
Overview

        
How we handle your coaching data

      

      

        AutoKnerd stores your profile, lessons, scores, and progress so the app can coach you over time. We use this data to power your dashboard, session history, profile, and role-specific lesson library.
      

      

        We may use anonymous coaching data to improve the product if you enable sharing in settings. Internal test tools stay hidden in developer mode unless you turn them on.
      

    


    

      

        
What we collect

        
Profile info, role, XP, lesson results, competency trends, and app preferences.

      

      

        
What we do not do

        
We do not sell your personal coaching data or expose internal test controls in the live build.

      TERMS OF USE
    

    

  


  

    

      

        
Agreement

        
Use the app responsibly

      

      

        AutoKnerd is a coaching and training app for dealership users. You agree to use the app for legitimate practice, coaching, and workflow support only.
      

      

        Session content, scores, and generated lesson plans are provided for coaching support. Do not rely on them as legal, financial, or employment advice.
      

    


    

      

        
Account access

        
Keep your login secure and do not share access with unauthorized users.

      

      

        
Content ownership

        
The app, coaching prompts, and generated lessons are part of the AutoKnerd service and may be updated over time.
